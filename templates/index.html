Plant Disease Library

Tomato Blight

A fungal disease that affects tomato plants, causing dark spots on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms:

  • Dark spots on leaves
  • Brown lesions on stems
  • Rotting fruit

Treatment:

  • Remove infected plants
  • Apply fungicide
  • Improve air circulation

Rose Black Spot

A common fungal disease that affects rose plants, causing black spots on leaves.

Symptoms:

  • Black spots on leaves
  • Yellowing of leaves
  • Premature leaf drop

Treatment:

  • Remove infected leaves
  • Apply fungicide spray
  • Water at the base of plants

Apple Scab

A fungal disease that affects apple trees, causing scabby lesions on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms:

  • Olive-green spots on leaves
  • Dark, scabby lesions on fruit
  • Premature leaf drop

Treatment:

  • Apply fungicide in spring
  • Prune infected branches
  • Clean up fallen leaves

Potato Late Blight

A devastating disease that affects potato plants, causing rapid plant death.

Symptoms:

  • Water-soaked lesions on leaves
  • White fungal growth
  • Rapid plant death

Treatment:

  • Remove infected plants
  • Apply copper fungicide
  • Plant resistant varieties

Grape Powdery Mildew

A fungal disease that affects grapevines, causing white powdery growth on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms:

  • White powdery patches
  • Stunted growth
  • Poor fruit quality

Treatment:

  • Apply sulfur fungicide
  • Improve air circulation
  • Prune infected parts

Citrus Canker

A bacterial disease that affects citrus trees, causing raised lesions on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms:

  • Raised lesions on leaves
  • Yellow halos around spots
  • Fruit drop

Treatment:

  • Remove infected trees
  • Apply copper spray
  • Control citrus psyllids
